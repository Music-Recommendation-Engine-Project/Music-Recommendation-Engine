Spotify Similar Artist Search

How it works:

 1. Click Login button below to Log into your Spotify.

 2. Skip the first step if you don't want to play music.

 3. The Login page will redirect you.

 4. Copy the URL of the redirected page.

 5. Type the name of your favorite artists.

 6. You'll get 5 recommended artist and top songs for each of them.

 7. Play Music and Enjoy!
